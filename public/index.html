About

The programmer behind the pipeline

I'm a Computer Science student at DigiPen Institute of Technology Singapore, specialising in real-time interactive simulation and graphics programming. Over three years I've shipped two custom game engines with my teams — integrating physics, building OpenGL rendering systems, and wiring up font rendering and 2D animation from scratch.

I'm comfortable working low in the stack: C++ memory management, x86-64 AT&T assembly, and the draw-call path from CPU to framebuffer. Before DigiPen I completed a Diploma in Electronics at Temasek Polytechnic, which gave me a grounding in hardware that shapes how I think about performance. I also teach as a TA for High-Level Programming and tutor first-year modules through the Academic Support Centre.
